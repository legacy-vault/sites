THE MAIN IDEA OF THE «МЭ» ALPHABET IS ENCLOSED IN THE FOLLOWING STATEMENTS:

1. ONE LETTER = ONE SOUND. { A = A }

2. SIMILAR LETTERS ≈ SIMILAR SOUNDS. { O ≈ Ö }

3. ASSOCIATIVE LOGICS. { ▲ ■ • }

IN OTHER WORDS, ONE LETTER IS READ AS IT IS WRITTEN, ONE LETTER HAS ONLY ONE SOUND, WITH NO EXCEPTIONS FROM THIS RULE. THOSE LETTERS WHICH HAVE SIMILAR SOUND HAVE SIMILAR INSCRIPTION. THE VISUAL IMAGE OF EACH LETTER IS MADE TO REMIND THE FORM AND POSITION OF THE SPEAKER'S VOCAL APPARATUS. THIS IDEA REMINDS ANCIENT HIEROGLYPHS OF THE MAYA, EGYPT AND OTHER CIVILIZATIONS, WHICH HAD THEIR OWN MEANING FOR EACH SYMBOL.

THE MAIN AIM OF THIS PHONETIC ALPHABET IS A TRUE AND UN-SPOILED RECORDING AND TRANSFER OF THE SINGING SOUNDS OF PEOPLE. MODERN LANGUAGES OF THE PRESENT DAY, EVEN THOSE LANGUAGES WHICH ARE WIDELY USED IN THE WORLD, HAVE BECOME VERY DISTORTED AND DIFFERENT FROM THEIR ORIGINS. IN MOST MODERN TONGUES THE WORDS ARE OFTEN BEING READ NOT AS THEY ARE WRITTEN — THE SAME LETTER HAS DIFFERENT SOUND IN DIFFERENT WORDS. THE EVIDENT EXAMPLE IS THE FIRST LATIN LETTER «A» — IT IS PRONOUNCED NOT AS IT USED TO BE PRONOUNCED IN THE PAST TIME.

«МЭ» ALPHABET CONSISTS OF SIX (6) GROUPS OF LETTERS AND SOUNDS:

1. MOOING;
2. BASIC OPEN SOUNDS;
3. SOFTENED OPEN SOUNDS;
4. COMPRESSED SOUNDS;
5. STOP SOUNDS;
6. SHORT SOUNDS.

IN «МЭ» THERE IS NO SUCH A DIVISION OF SOUNDS INTO TWO GROUPS (VOWELS AND CONSONANTS) AS IN MODERN LANGUAGES. IN «МЭ» SOUNDS ARE CLASSIFIED INTO GROUPS ACCORDING TO THE WAY OF THEIR CREATION, MORE COMPLICATED THAN IN ORDINARY LANGUAGE.

«MOOING» GROUP OF SOUNDS CONTAINS THOSE SOUNDS WHICH ARE PRONOUNCED WITH THE MOUTH CLOSED OR NEARLY CLOSED. «OPEN SOUNDS» (TWO GROUPS) ARE PRONOUNCED WITH MOUTH WIDE OPEN. «COMPRESSED SOUNDS» ARE THOSE SOUNDS WHICH ARE CREATED BY THE VARIOUS TYPES OF COMPRESSION, DISTORTION, OSCILLATION OR VIBRATION OF THE AIR FLOW WHICH COMES OUT OF THE SPEAKER'S VOCAL APPARATUS OR IS INSIDE THE SPEAKER'S MOUTH. «STOP SOUNDS» ARE THE SOUNDS WHICH PARTIALLY OT FULLY STOP THE AIR FLOW FROM OR INSIDE THE SPEAKER'S MOUTH. «SHORT SOUNDS» ARE JUST SHORT TYPES OF SOUNDS, AS THE NAME SAYS.

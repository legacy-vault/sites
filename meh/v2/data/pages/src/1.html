THIS ALPHABET HAS BEEN CREATED TO RECORD AND TRANSFER SINGING OF PEOPLE'S VOICES.

YOU MAY ASK, WHAT THE PHONETIC ALPHABET «МЭ» IS AND WHAT IS ALL THIS FOR ? THE ANSWER IS SIMPLE. THIS ALPHABET HAS BEEN CREATED TO UNIFY DIFFERENT LANGUAGES AND TO MAKE ONE ALPHABET WHICH DOES NOT DISTORT THE SOUNDS. IT IS KNOWN THAT MODERN LANGUAGES IN THEIR PROCESS OF EVOLUTION HAVE CHANGED THEMSELVES SO MUCH THAT THEY HAVE LOST THE SENSE OF THEIR ORIGIN. THE SAME LETTER HAS MORE THAN ONE SOUNDS AND MORE THAN ONE MEANING IN THE MODERN TONGUES.

ONE SIMPLE EXAMPLE IS THE LATIN LETTER «A». IN THE WORD «PARK» IT HAS ONE SOUND, IN THE WORD «MAP» IT SOUNDS IN A DIFFERENT WAY. MOREOVER, IN THE WORD «MATE» IT HAS THIRD PRONOUNCIATION. ANCIENT LANGUAGES WERE MORE STRICT IN THIS QUESTION AND ONE LETTER MEANED ONLY ONE EXACT SOUND AT THAT TIME.

THIS «МЭ» PHONETIC ALPHABET HAS BEEN CONSTRUCTED IN ORDER TO MAKE AN ORGANIC AND LIVE TOOL, WHICH EASILY MAKES ASSOCIATIONS IN MIND. THE ALPHABET USES SIMPLE AND MEMORABLE FORMS OF SYMBOLS THAT ARE MORE NATURAL TO HUMAN BEING THAN EXISTING TOOLS.

IF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN A SIMPLE AND, AT THE SAME TIME, UNIVERSAL VOICE SCRIPTING TOOL (LATIN ORIGIN: SCRIPTUM), YOU ARE WELCOME TO THIS WEBSITE.

PLEASE, DO NOT BE SURPRISED WHEN YOU SEE THE TEXT IN CAPITAL LETTERS. THIS HAS BEEN DONE INTENTIONALLY. IN THE PAST TIME, OLD AND ANCIENT WRITINGS AND SCRIPTINGS WERE DONE IN HIEROGLYPHS, WHICH WERE OF THE SAME SIZE. THIS WEBSITE TRIES TO RECOVER THAT OLD TRADITION INTO THE MODERN WORLD.
